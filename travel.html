Venue

After 3 years since its inception with just a handful of members, the DMV Based Rhythm Gaming Community is ambitiously hosting it’s very first official rhythm gaming tournament in the East Coast from December 10th – December 12th, 2021. The first event will be proudly hosted at the Baltimore Convention Center, just a mere few blocks away from the iconic Inner Harbor that hosts a countless amount of annual large-scale events settled in the heart of the Port of Baltimore.

Airports

The closest Airport to Baltimore will be the Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport, located 10 miles south of the venue.

Getting To The Venue

BWI has an abundance of Uber & Lyft drivers within minutes of the airport as an option, it is the quickest transit, but my not be the cheapest. If you so please, you can save some money by taking the BWI Airport Light Rail NB Station directly from the airport to the Camden Station Light Rail, right in front of the Baltimore Orioles Ballpark, which is 0.2 miles away or one block from the Convention Center. The Light Rail link runs every 20 mins.
